Classes

Welcome to the classes page. We offer classes for all ranges of skill level and motorcycles. Click the buttons on the left to learn about each class.

Open Pro

This class is Unlimited Displacement, Unlimited modifications. Open to any skill level.

450

This class is unlimited modifications, Up to 450cc liquid cooled, four stroke. Open displacement air cooled single, Up to 450cc 4 stroke and up to 300cc two stroke.

250/Lightweight Amateur

Unlimited modification for, Up to 200cc 2 strokes, 2-stroke Single , Up to 250cc air-cooled, 2-stroke Single, Up to 300cc liquid-cooled 4-stroke Single, Up to 400cc air-cooled, 4-stroke Single. Also factory street legal motorcycles of Unlimited Displacement or knobby shod dirt bikes in stock trim (pipe allowed.)

Asphalt Only

This class is Unlimited Displacement, Unlimited modifications, open to any skill level, no dirt.

Intermediate

Open Displacement with Unlimited modifications, open to any rider that has never podiumed the Open class or held an AMA Pro Supermoto license.

Novice

This class is Unlimited Displacement, Unlimited modifications, open to Novice riders only. Season ending bumps will be done on a mark system.

Sportsman

Unlimited Displacement factory street legal Supermoto bikes. Any off road/mx model with OEM wheel sizes, aftermarket brakes allowed.

Women's

Unlimited Displacement, Unlimited modifications, open to any skill level.
